learn

A series of lessons that slowly build up your knowledge of the keyboard’s layout. Each part of the keyboard is split up into stages. Many untimed tasks introduce you the keys one by one, where you must complete the sentences without using up all your ‘chances’ (the blue circles). Followed by a timed challenge at the end as a kind of boss before moving on to the next part. After completing a few levels you will unlock..

About

TypEase is the first bit of functioning javascript created by Ben Green. It was designed to help him learn to type faster and ease the road to becoming a developer.


It is written in html, scss and javascript with jquery, using no other external libraries. Many dead ends still hold his footprints, but each one is valuable, shaped by the kind of challenges beginners need to chew through in order to learn.


Ben hopes you enjoy it and have a little play through.
